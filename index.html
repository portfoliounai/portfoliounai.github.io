LEINN - Entrepreneurial Leadership & Innovation



LEINN (Entrepreneurial Leadership and Innovation) is the official university degree taught by the Mondragon University business school, whose objective is the training of professionals capable of developing innovative projects that meet the needs of globalized markets.

We acquire the skills through the development of real projects: Team based learning, leading in a team and team entrepreneurship. This allows us to understand and interpret knowledge: Companies and their environment, instrumental knowledge and company creation and management.

Among others, it trains in the following skills:
• Project management
• Team learning and leadership techniques
• Creativity and innovation
• International world view
• Communication and self-management skills

MTA - Mondragon Team Academy


We belong to Mondragon Team Academy (MTA). It is an international community with more than 2,500 team entrepreneurs helping to create a global network of social innovation ecosystem labs.

The 21 skills encompassed by the programme's curriculum have been tested in practical situations for more than 15 years by Team Academy in Finland and they meet the need to learn, lead and engage in entrepreneurship within a collaborative context.

KANDA TEAM COMPANY

16 young entrepreneurs set up Kanda Team Company when we began our studies. It has been our vehicle for learning and personal and team transformation. We have been able to experiment, learn and share as a team, developing entrepreneurial projects through this legal entity.

Following the "Learning by Doing" methodology, we have created a team where action-oriented learning and shared leadership are combined, developing ourselves as "teampreneurs, teamleaders and teamlearners".

With Kanda, I have developed professional skills and personal skills working on the implementation of real projects and services, interacting with companies and entities developing innovative projects co-creating with our clients.

It has also allowed me to learn and experiment in international markets, creating contacts and working on skills such as planning, business model design, product and service development, user experience, and skills such as leadership, negotiation, decision making and dialogue.
